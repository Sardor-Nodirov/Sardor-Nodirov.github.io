AS 172 Project 1 - Saturn by Cassini & Huygens

Press the green flag to start the game. The music will sound which you can turn off by clicking on the speaker icon. You can choose to play a story (slideshow-style) with interesting facts about Saturn, or you can play an interactive game "Catch-a-Planet". In this game, you must find and click on the correct planet whose name is displayed on the screen. Good luck and have fun!
